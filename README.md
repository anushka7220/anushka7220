### About

I am an Applied AI Engineer working at the intersection of messy real world data and reliable system design. My focus is on semantic search pipelines, knowledge graphs, multi model inference, and retrieval augmented workflows that are engineered to perform under real production load.

I build the infrastructure around AI, not just on top of it: async backends that stay up under load, pipelines that replace entire manual workflows, and ML systems built from first principles when no off the shelf solution exists. One example: designing a low resource speech recognition system for a language with zero viable existing tooling, grounded in classical phonetic theory rather than brute forced with data.

Currently building production LLM systems at Memeraki Pvt. Ltd.

### What I Have Shipped

**Semantic SEO Engine.** A seven stage pipeline using Sentence Transformers and Gemini 2.5 Flash that fully automated anchor tag injection across 1,300+ pages. Eliminated 10 hours per week of manual editorial work and improved internal link graph coverage by 80%.

**Neo4j Knowledge Graph.** A graph of 10,000+ entities and 74,000+ relationships spanning blogs, artforms, artists, themes, and products, enabling graph based content discovery and semantic retrieval.

**Low Resource Sanskrit ASR.** An acoustic speech recognition pipeline built from phonetic first principles for a language with no viable off the shelf solution. Custom phoneme level dataset, fully automated preprocessing, and a TensorFlow classifier reaching 90% accuracy on data that did not exist six months earlier.

**Samachar Vani.** A multimodal pipeline combining YOLOv8, EasyOCR, and an LLM to convert scanned Hindi newspapers into podcast style audio summaries, served through an async FastAPI backend with authentication and background job scheduling.

**Review Pipeline Automation.** Replaced a paid third party review tool with an Apify, n8n, and Firestore pipeline. Cut costs by $30 to $40 per month and reduced review turnaround from weeks to under 24 hours.
